AnyControl

  • Kai Wedekind
  • 26 Jul 2018
  • 02 Jan 2020

AnyControl is a small JavaScript SpeechRecognition library that lets your users control your site with voice commands. It is build on top of Webkit Speech API.

AnyControl has no dependencies, just 5 KB small, and is free to use and modify under the MIT license.

Setup

Add script to your site

      <script src="https://unpkg.com/anycontrol/dist/index.umd.min.js"></script>

Examples

  • Alexa, can you go to the next page

  • Now go back to the previous page

  • Hello Siri, send a message to Michael saying 'Hi there!' and send it at 2 p.m.

  • Alexa, what day is it?

  • Alexa, what is 13 times 3?

  • Siri, who is the president of America?

  • Siri, who is the president of Argentina?

  • Siri, who is the president of China?
